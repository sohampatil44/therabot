Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Therabot.

  • What is Therabot?

    Therabot is a cozy, AI-driven chatbot designed to provide empathetic therapeutic conversation and mental wellbeing support. It's here to listen and offer guidance based on your input.

  • How does Therabot work?

    It uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) models to understand the emotion and context in your messages. Based on this, it retrieves relevant information and generates a thoughtful, supportive response using AI.

  • Is my data safe and private?

    Your privacy is important. Conversations are processed securely. While we use advanced models, remember Therabot is not a replacement for professional human therapy. User authentication protects your session.

  • Can Therabot diagnose conditions?

    No, Therabot is an AI assistant for emotional support and conversation. It cannot provide medical diagnoses, treatment plans, or crisis intervention. If you need medical advice or are in crisis, please contact a qualified healthcare professional or a crisis hotline immediately.
